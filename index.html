  • Outside Opportunities

Uniqueness

The college admission process is getting more competitive each year. Through Gwinnett Tri-M, you can stand out as a musical ambassador on your college application--making yourself a unique individual with a love for the arts while helping others.

  • Does Music help with College?

Service

What does service through music look like? Musical volunteering includes performances at retirement homes, shelters, and more. If you aren't a fan of public performances, there are opportunities for you to set up concerts, tutor students, and help fine arts teachers. ALL of which will go towards volunteering hours that you can utilize for your impressive resume.

Competitions

Get recognized and rewarded for your exceptional musical talent by competing in upcoming competitions in the Gwinnett Tri-M Honor Society. Prizes will be monetary and/or concerto performances.
