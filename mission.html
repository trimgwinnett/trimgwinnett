The Mission

Through the unification of everyone's passion for music attributed with the love to serve the community across Gwinnett County, the Gwinnett Tri-M Honor Society formalizes these aspirations into an available and distributable organization. With upcoming events, future elections, performing solos/ensembles and so much more, the possibilities are endless. Being apart of Tri-M recognizes the excellency achieved in music and academics, so be proud and confidently serve the community, creating a tailored experience to remember for the rest of your life!
